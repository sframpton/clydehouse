Cancellation Policy

Please remember that when you confirm your accommodation booking you are entering into a legal contract. If you alter your travel plans, please advise Clyde House immediately, whereupon the booking can be cancelled by mutual agreement. If an agreement is not possible, Clyde House is entitled to claim compensation for failure to fulfill the booking.

Cancellation must be made by 6.00pm, three days prior to the arrival date, after which time bookings will be subject to 100% of the accommodation tariff being charged for the duration of your booking.

We recommend when booking any accommodation, holiday insurance is taken out.
