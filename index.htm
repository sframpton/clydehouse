Welcome to Clyde House

Sean and Heather Flynn welcome you to Clyde House, our 18th Century guest house in Leyburn, North Yorkshire. We are sure you will enjoy your stay with us, we look forward to welcoming you.

Customer information from Clyde House

Clyde House plans to re-open for business on Satuday 4th July, in line with government guidelines.

There will of course be a few changes:

We will only be using three of our five rooms to start with during these difficult times. Our new policy for this period will be a minimum stay of two nights, to allow us to clean each room thoroughly. When making a reservation, please ring or email to speak to Sean or Heather personally, so that we can inform you of all the changes we have implemented

For more details about the changes we are implementing, please click here.

Heather and Sean will aim to respond to all booking requests within 48 hours in order to confirm your booking and arrange for any deposit payment required. We are very much looking forward to re-opening and welcoming everyone in these unusual circumstances. Thank you for co-operation and we both look forward to welcoming to Clyde House.
