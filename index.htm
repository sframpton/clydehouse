Welcome to Clyde House

Sean and Heather Flynn welcome you to Clyde House, our 18th Century guest house in Leyburn, North Yorkshire. We are sure you will enjoy your stay with us, we look forward to welcoming you.

It is now possible to make an online booking enquiry through the 'Book Now' option that appears in the menu on the website. You can access the menu by clicking on the icon in the top left hand corner (it looks like three back lines stacked together). Selecting the 'Book Now' option from the menu, will take you to our new online reservation centre which will show real-time information about availability in all our rooms. Once you select your chosen arrival date and length of stay, the rooms available to book at Clyde House are shown along with the room rate. You can then select the room you require and an email will be sent to Heather and Sean with your booking request.

Heather and Sean will aim to respond to all booking requests within 48 hours in order to confirm your booking and arrange for any deposit payment required. Any dates and rooms selected through the online system will be held for you pending confirmation.
